Data Protection Commitment

At The Legacy Box Collection, we treat your personal information with the same exclusivity and care as our boxes. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

We collect only essential information necessary for your Legacy Box experience:

Premium Privacy: We never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data remains exclusively within The Legacy Box Collection ecosystem.

How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to provide exceptional service:

  • Enhance your experience with personalized communications

Financial Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures for all financial transactions:

  • SSL encryption for all data transfers
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • Secure storage of financial records for revenue distribution
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

Cookies & Tracking

Session Management Site Performance Preference Storage
